CardScan-apotamus Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Overview

CardScan-apotamus ("the App") is a business card scanning app developed by Steven Bliss. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

Camera Access

The App uses your device's camera to scan business cards. Images are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework for text recognition. No images or scanned data are sent to any server.

Photo Library Access

The App may request access to your photo library to import business card images. Selected images are processed on-device only.

Contacts Access

The App may request access to your contacts to save scanned business card information. Contact data is written directly to your device's address book and is never transmitted externally.

Data Storage

Scanned card data is stored locally on your device. No data is stored on external servers, cloud services, or third-party platforms.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our App Store listing.
