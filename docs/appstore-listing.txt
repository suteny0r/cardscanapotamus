--- SUBTITLE (30 characters max) ---

Business Card Scanner & Export


--- PROMOTIONAL TEXT (170 characters max, can be updated without a new build) ---

Scan business cards instantly with your camera or import from photos and files. Export to Excel, save to Contacts, and tag cards by source — all on your iPhone.


--- DESCRIPTION (4000 characters max) ---

CardScan-apotamus turns your iPhone into a powerful business card scanner. Snap a photo, and advanced OCR instantly extracts names, titles, companies, phone numbers, emails, websites, and full mailing addresses — no typing required.

SCAN YOUR WAY
• Camera: Take a photo with a zoomed-in camera optimized for business cards
• Photo Library: Import card images you've already captured
• Files: Pick card images from iCloud Drive or other file providers
• QR Codes: Automatically reads vCard QR codes on business cards for perfect accuracy

SMART FIELD EXTRACTION
The built-in OCR engine intelligently parses business card text into structured fields:
• Name, job title, and company
• Email address and website (including international domains)
• Up to 3 phone numbers with automatic Phone, Cell, and Fax type detection
• Full mailing address broken into street, city, state, zip, and country
• International address and phone format support

Every field is fully editable — review and correct anything before saving.

ORGANIZE WITH SOURCES
Tag each card with a source like a trade show, conference, or event name. Create and manage source tags right from the app. Filter your card list by source to stay organized.

EXPORT TO EXCEL
Share your scanned cards as a professionally formatted .xlsx spreadsheet. All fields are included: name, title, company, email, phone, cell, fax, website, full address, source, notes, and date scanned. Perfect for importing into your CRM or contact management system.

SAVE TO CONTACTS
Add scanned cards directly to your iPhone Contacts with one tap. Review the contact card before saving — no surprises.

BUILT FOR PRIVACY
CardScan-apotamus processes everything on your device. Your business card data is never uploaded to external servers. No account required. No ads. No tracking.

ADDITIONAL FEATURES
• Card image saved with each scan for reference
• Notes field for adding context to any card
• Delete individual cards or clear all at once
• Swipe to delete from the card list
• Card thumbnails in the list view for quick identification

===========================================================
 SUGGESTED SCREENSHOTS
===========================================================

App Store requires 6.7" (iPhone 15 Pro Max / 16 Pro Max)
and optionally 6.5" and 5.5" sizes. Aim for 5-8 screenshots.
They appear in order, so lead with the most compelling.

1. CARD LIST (main screen)
   - Show 4-6 scanned cards with thumbnails, names, companies,
     and at least one source tag badge visible
   - Demonstrates the app's core value at a glance

2. CAMERA SCANNING
   - Show the camera view with a business card in frame
   - The zoomed-in viewfinder with the capture button visible

3. SCAN OPTIONS
   - Show the "Scan a Business Card" prompt screen with all
     three options: Take Photo, Choose from Photos, Choose
     from Files

4. CARD DETAIL — CONTACT INFO (top half)
   - Show a fully populated card after scanning
   - Card image at top, name/title/company fields filled in
   - Shows the quality of OCR extraction

5. CARD DETAIL — CONTACT DETAILS (scrolled down)
   - Show phone numbers with type dropdowns (Phone/Cell/Fax),
     email, website, and structured address fields
   - Demonstrates the depth of data capture

6. SAVE TO CONTACTS
   - Show the iOS contact card preview that appears when
     tapping "Save to Contacts"
   - Proves seamless integration with iPhone Contacts

7. EXPORT SHARE SHEET
   - Show the share sheet that appears after tapping the
     export button, with "ScannedCards.xlsx" visible
   - Demonstrates the Excel export feature

8. SOURCE TAGGING
   - Show the source picker bar at the top of the main screen
     with a source selected and the manage sources view
   - Demonstrates the organizational capability

SCREENSHOT TIPS:
- Use real (or realistic) business card data, not placeholder text
- Ensure cards show a variety of companies and names
- Show at least one card with a source tag in the list view
- Consider using a status bar showing full signal and battery
- Keep the status bar time clean (e.g., 9:41 — Apple's
  preferred screenshot time)
